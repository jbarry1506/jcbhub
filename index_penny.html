Welcome to Scaredy Pi!

Welcome to year two of the WildBarry Halloween Party!!!
This year we've decided to have a little more scary fun with clowns, lol. As you can see, there are pictures being changed up periodically.
This is happening through a series of events triggered from a simple button push. The button kicks off a blast of cold air from a leafblower (held by the clown you see above), lights up a creepy clown with glowing eyes, triggers a scream, and takes a little snapshot to remember the occasion. Then, the picture gets pushed to source control.
I have a jenkins server that is polling my repository every minute for changes. If there is a change, this website is rebuilt. The pictures are being stored, so if you want to see the silly face you made, just ask, lol.
LOVE YOU GUYS!!! Happy Halloween!!!
